My name is Doda and I am a passionate and avid Barbie collector. I built this site to grow a heavenly doll place for me and other Barbie fanatics. At DodaDolls you can find pictures of my collection, one of a kind doll creations, hybrids, rarities, as well as diorama/DIY inspirations and doll art. My DollFinder shall assist your search for your grail doll.

When I was 8 years old I fell in love with Mattel's Jewel Secrets Whitney and made a silent promise to myself:
"When I grow up, I shall get all the Barbies I always dreamed of."
Over 20 years later I can look at a small but solid collection of boxed and unboxed Steffie mold dolls, Superstar Barbies and modern Fashionistas. It gives me enormous joy to photograph and pose my hybrid dolls or create rooms for their doll events. You can find my deboxing videos on YouTube. Let me invite you on a colourful trip into the unique world of DodaDolls!
