DodaDolls interview on Barbie Dolls of the Week **February 2021** - As you may know, I have been collecting on and off for over 20 years, with the online market being one of the biggest sources of my middle sized Barbie collection. So it is not a coincidence that I discovered Rann's channel online as soon as I wanted to know more about certain Barbie dolls. **I am very happy to have gotten the chance to sit down and talk to Rann**, the creator and host of "Barbie Dolls of the Week", a YouTube channel dedicated to the contemporary adult collector. It is a great source of information and one of the few qualitative channels that inspired me to dig deeper into everything Barbie and that accompanied me while I was restarting my collection in 2017. ### The topics we get into are (among others): * Steffie dolls (my favorite face mold) * Importing dolls from the US * DIY ideas for dolls * What started my collection Feel free to participate in the discussion on YouTube!
